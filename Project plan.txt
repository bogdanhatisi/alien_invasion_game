Alien Invasion is a game in which the player controls a rocket ship that is located at the bottom center of the screen.The player can board the ship right and left and shoot bullets by pressing the spacebar.At the beginning of the game, an alien fleet fills the "sky" and moves across and down the screen.The player's objective is to shoot and destroy the aliens.If the player shoots all the aliens, a new fleet appears that moves faster than the previous one.If any alien hits the player's ship or reaches the bottom of the screen, the player loses a life.If the player loses three lives, the game ends.
